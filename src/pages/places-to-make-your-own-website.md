They all free to use. but not all free to host your website on. but some of them provides a good free plans.

### Websites Builders and Hosters
- [Cargo](https://cargo.site/)
- [Framer](https://www.framer.com/solutions/website-builder/)
- [mmm.page](https://mmm.page/)
- [Studio Design](https://studio.design/store/all)
- [Wordpress.org](https://wordpress.org/) (this provides a self hosted software to build your website, but to host it you need to go to [wordpress.com](https://wordpress.com/))

### Only Static Websites Hosters
- [neocities](https://neocities.org/)
- [nekoweb](https://nekoweb.org/)
